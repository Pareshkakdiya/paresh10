President of india

Smt. Droupadi Murmu was sworn in as india 15th President.
She succeeded Ram Nath Kovind, whose term ended in July 2022.

Smt. Droupadi Murmu was born into a Santali tribal family on 30 june 1958, in Odisha.


 She grew up in a village where getting primary education was like a dream. Her childhood was 
    full of challenges. She became the first girl from her village to join a colleage.
